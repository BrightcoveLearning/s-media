Using the Correct Ingest Profile

In order to use the Video Editor, your content should have "Video Editing-MP4" renditions.
There are multiple ways to achieve this:

MP4 renditions are needed to use the video editor.
"Video Editing-MP4" renditions are specifically optimized to perform better with the video editor.

Upload a video with the Video Editing-MP4 Ingest profle:

When uploading your video, select the Video Editing-MP4ingest profile.

Adding the MP4 renditions:

To add the MP4 renditions needed to work with the video editor follow the next steps:

  1. Navigate to the Media module.
  2. Select the video you would like to work with.
  3. Scroll down to the Video Files section and click Create MP4

  4. Select Video Editing-MP4 as your ingest profile

  5. Click Create

Retranscoding your video:

You can retranscode an existing video from your video library, selecting Video Editing-MP4 as your ingest profile.

Editing an existing video

To start editing an existing video follow the next steps:

  1. Navigate to the Media module.
  2. Select the video you would like to work with.
  3. Click Open in Editor

  4. Click Continue
  5. After this process, you will see the video editor.
